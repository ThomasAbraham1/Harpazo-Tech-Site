## Copywriting Framework Used
> **Problem → Proof → Promise** (via Harry Dry principles)
> - Lead with the client's pain, not your features.
> - Use specific, real numbers over vague claims.
> - Write toward who you're becoming, while being honest about where you are.

## Copywriting Notes

- **Avoid:** "world-class", "cutting-edge", "innovative", "full-service", "seamless"
- **Use instead:** Specific numbers, timelines, plain outcomes
- **Tone:** Conversational, confident, honest about startup stage
- **Specificity rule:** A specific small number ("4 clients") beats a vague large claim ("many clients") every time
